## Typography
- **Headlines**: Space Grotesk (wght 300-700)
- **Body**: Manrope (wght 300-700)
- **Labels**: Manrope

## Effects
- Glassmorphism: `backdrop-filter: blur(24px)`
- Orbs: Animated background orbs with blur(120px)
- Borders: 1px solid rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.1)

## Components
- Header: Fixed, backdrop-blur-xl, border-b
- Stats Cards: Glass effect, hover states
- Agent Cards: Horizontal layout, load bars
- Buttons: Gradient backgrounds, rounded-2xl
